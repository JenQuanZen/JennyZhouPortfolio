Artist Statement

Complexity. You can find it everywhere, if you're keeping an eye out for it. Complexity in the world around me inspires my creativity. I easily get lost in the infinite details and diversity of people, animals, and nature. Everyone has their own unique story to tell, and depending on who's telling it, the story can transform into a multitude of perspectives.


Visual storytelling is my passion. Or maybe obsession. If I'm recounting an episode from class to a friend, I'm the kind of person that will incorporate the pile of clementines on the table as "characters" in the story. I learned during my study abroad that this is the scientific process of "doll scenarios" that they often use for participatory design research.


Stories have the ability to both create escapist experiences as well as present a new understanding of the world around us. They are both a means of expression and a device to enlighten the audience. I love diving into the messy duality of things, exploring the difference and entanglement of "good" and "bad" within characters and story. Much of my work is almost a meditation of sorts, a reflection of my experiences onto alternative or fantastical worlds. I'm a perfectionist who's intrigued by the imperfection and messiness of life.


I'm an aspiring Storyboard Artist with experience in media, communications, and programming. I coded this portfolio website to highlight my skillsets as well as to create a user friendly navigation experience.
